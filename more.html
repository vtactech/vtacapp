What is this ?

A program to see the world without eyes , A sixth sense .


Who should use it ?

Any person who wish to have some superhero powers or a sixth sense , or a visually disabled person .


Why is it made ?

To give visually disabled person a ability to see the world without the need of eyes .


How to use ?

Tap the switch button to turn scanner On / Off .


Sound patterns :-


Repeating same pattern Sound the objects around you is constant , So if you hear the same pattern again , it's most likely you are the same place in the same position . (or in some cases a photo of that exact place is placed in front of you .)


No Repeating pattern Sound Either your device ( or you ) are moving or the objects around you is moving or both or all three .


No Sound It's complete dark or the program is switched off , try reloading page , pressing switch button and make sure light is present in the room .


Volume of Sound decreasing you are moving to a dimmer place


Volume of Sound increasing you are moving to a lighter place


Same sound :There is a wall very near to you technically same colour all over the place
